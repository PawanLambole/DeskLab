Frequently Asked Questions

Is DeskLab really free?

Yes! DeskLab is completely free and open source. No subscriptions, no hidden fees.

How secure is DeskLab?

Very secure. We use direct peer-to-peer connections with WebRTC encryption. Your data never goes through our servers.

Do I need to install on both PCs?

Yes, both the host (person sharing screen) and viewer need to install DeskLab.

Can I use it over the internet?

Currently optimized for same-network use. Internet support with ngrok or port forwarding is documented.

How do updates work?

DeskLab automatically checks for updates on startup. When available, you get a notification with one-click install.

What platforms are supported?

Currently Windows 10/11 (64-bit). macOS and Linux support is planned for future releases.
